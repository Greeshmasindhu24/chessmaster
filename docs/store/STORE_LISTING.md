## Full description (max 4000 characters)

```
ChessMaster Pro is your pocket chess club — play live games against real opponents, challenge AI at multiple skill levels, or practice on a local board whenever you have a few minutes.

♔ LIVE MULTIPLAYER
Find opponents instantly or invite friends with a room code. Real-time move sync, clocks, in-game chat, draw offers, and resignation — all powered by secure WebSocket connections.

🤖 PLAY VS AI
Sharpen your skills against computer opponents. Unlock higher difficulty tiers as you improve (in-app purchases coming in future updates).

📋 GAME HISTORY
Track wins, losses, and draws on your dashboard. Review recent results and watch your record grow.

⚙️ YOUR PROFILE
Customize board themes, manage your profile, and adjust preferences. Sign in with email to sync your games across sessions.

🎯 BUILT FOR MOBILE
Smooth, responsive board designed for touch. Dark theme optimized for comfortable play day or night.

FEATURES IN THIS RELEASE
• Email registration and secure login
• Real-time online chess with matchmaking
• Private games via room codes
• AI opponents (Beginner tier free)
• Local practice board
• Move validation and persisted game records
• Profile and appearance settings

COMING SOON
• Google Sign-In
• Advanced puzzles and game analysis
• Tournaments and leaderboards
• Friends and notifications

ChessMaster Pro connects to our secure cloud API for accounts and multiplayer. An internet connection is required for online play and sign-in. Local board mode works offline after the app is loaded.

Questions or feedback? Contact us at support@chessmaster.pro

Privacy Policy: https://chessmaster-web.onrender.com/privacy
Terms of Service: https://chessmaster-web.onrender.com/terms
```
